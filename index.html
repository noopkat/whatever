Projects

  • Open Source Fun

    Open Source Fun is a Twitch to YouTube live coding experience, which guides viewers through the experience of what it's like to write and maintain open source software for hardware and internet of things. Selected videos are edited, captioned, and released on YouTube shortly after the Twitch cast.

  • Owl Posse

    Owl Posse is a 3D printed contemporary jewellery range designed by Suz Hinton. Inspired by a little bit of steampunk, and a little bit of fantasy, her designs surprise and delight those young at heart. The collection is now retired and unavailable for purchase.

  • AVRGirl

    The avrgirl project provides a NodeJS alternative to interacting with Atmel® AVR microchips. Compatible with a wide variety of Atmel® products, and the Arduino platform.

  • Oled-js

    Oled-js is a fully JavaScript driven library for controlling SSD1306 OLED screens for hardware projects. Write text, images, lines and shapes in real time.

  • Meow Machine

    Meow Machine is a blog dedicated to technical explorations and showcasing personal projects. Skewed towards educational content, Suz helps others understand programming, and creative ideas.

  • Meow Shoes

    Musical sensor enabled shoes project. Force sensitive resisters placed at the heel and toe of each shoe communicate via an Arduino serial interface to sequence musical loops in the browser.

  • Tobias

    Voice recognition and the Giphy API unite to create a unique story telling experience. Tell Tobias a story, and he'll annotate it with GIF's in your browser window.

  • P.U.R.R. bracelet

    Personal Ultimate Reassurance Response bracelet. Monitors your pulse, and if you're having a sustained anxiety attack, it'll send you kitten photos via text message.

  • 3D Print Weekly

    A retired (but still accessible) weekly newsletter with curated articles related to 3D printing. 67 editions were published in total.

  • Lathe-R-Beam

    An interactive lathe inspired modeling tool in the browser, written in JavaScript. Easily create models for exporting and 3D printing by drawing a simple silhouette. Made in collaboration with @makenai.
